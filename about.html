We're a small, deliberate team of designers, strategists, and builders. We believe in doing fewer things at a higher standard — and we're unreasonably particular about the details.

Our Story

AXIOM Studio was founded in 2018 by creative director Nadia Holt and strategist Elliot Marsh, who met while leading projects at a global agency in London. They shared a frustration: the larger the agency, the further the work drifted from the original idea.

They left to build something smaller, more intentional. The name came from a philosophy: an axiom is a truth that doesn't require proof. The brands they wanted to build would be the same — self-evident, undeniable.

The studio grew quietly at first, working with a handful of early-stage companies who couldn't afford a large agency but needed the quality of one. Word spread. Within two years, they were turning away more projects than they accepted.

Today, AXIOM Studio operates from studios in London and Cape Town with a core team of eight and a trusted network of specialists. We still turn work away. Every project is a commitment — to the client, to the idea, and to the work itself.

What We Stand For

Principles that
don't flex.

Precision

We sweat the details that most people never notice — because the people who do notice are usually the ones worth impressing. Craft is not decoration; it's how we communicate respect for the work and the audience.

Courage

Safe work is invisible work. We push for ideas that carry risk — and we back our clients through the discomfort. Agreeing with everyone is not a design philosophy; it's a failure of imagination.

Clarity

We remove until we can't remove anymore. A brand with too many ideas has no idea. Clarity is not simplicity — it is the hard-won absence of noise, so the right signal reaches the right person.

Partnership

We don't take briefs; we form alliances. The best outcomes happen when both sides bring full knowledge, full honesty, and full commitment to the table. We work with clients who treat us as collaborators, not vendors.

Longevity

Trend-chasing is expensive. We design for the long arc — systems built to flex and grow, identities that age well, and digital products that don't require a rebuild every eighteen months.

Curiosity

We stay students. Design, technology, and culture move fast. The studio that stops learning stops being useful. We invest in exploration — even when there's no immediate project to apply it to.
